## Ryan Tasseff, Ph.D. — Program Operational Lead

**Current role:** Data Officer, CIC biomaGUNE (Donostia-San Sebastian, Spain)

**Full CV:** See `RyanTasseff_CV.md`

### Short bio (proposal-ready, ~150 words)

Dr. Ryan Tasseff is Data Officer at CIC biomaGUNE, where he manages a multi-node biomedical imaging network (ReDIB/ICTS), leads data management strategy, and identifies opportunities in AI-driven image analysis and multimodal data integration. He holds a Ph.D. in Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering from Cornell University (NSF IGERT Fellow) and completed postdoctoral work at the Institute for Systems Biology. His career spans academic research (systems biology, computational modeling, clinical genomics with 2000+ genomes), industry leadership at Procter & Gamble (Group Head, $1M+ budget, cross-disciplinary teams, patented oral care technologies), and executive roles (CSO at Biocellion SPC). He has 30+ publications in journals including PNAS, PLoS Computational Biology, and Scientific Reports, and holds a US patent. His combined expertise in computational biology, mechanistic modeling (nonlinear dynamics, statistical mechanics, ODE/PDE systems), AI-driven imaging, data infrastructure, team management, and translational science uniquely positions him to lead a program bridging institutional capability with theory-infused AI.
